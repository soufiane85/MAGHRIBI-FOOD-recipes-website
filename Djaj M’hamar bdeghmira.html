Rfissa

Ingredients:

    For the chicken:

  1. 1 ½ kg whole chicken with the skin on, cleaned
  2. 3 garlic cloves, peeled
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  5. ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  6. 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  7. A pinch of saffron threads, soaked in 2 tablespoons water
  8. 2 tablespoons unsalted butter (at room temperature)

    For the onion sauce:

  1. 2 large yellow onions
  2. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  3. 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  4. 1 small bouquet parsley and coriander
  5. ¼ preserved lemon (pulp and peel)
  6. 1 teaspoon smen (preserved butter)
  7. 3 tablespoons olive oil
  8. 250 ml water

    For serving:

  1. 1 Preserved lemon peel
  2. A handful Red olives (cured)
