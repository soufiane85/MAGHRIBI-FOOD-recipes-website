Moroccan Mint Tea

Ingredients:

  1. 7 fresh mint sprigs cut in half so they can fit in the teapot and more for garnish.
  2. 1 heaped teaspoon loose gunpowder tea.
  3. 500ml boiling water.
  4. tablespoons caster sugar.

Instructions:

  1. Add tea + ½ cup boiling water → simmer → keep liquid (essence)
  2. Rinse tea leaves with ½ cup boiling water → discard rinse
  3. Add mint, sugar, and essence back into pot
  4. Fill with remaining boiling water → let slowly boil
  5. Stir to dissolve sugar → serve hot (use strainer if needed)
  6. Optional: fresh mint in each glass for aroma & decoration
