Moroccan Couscous

Ingredients:

  1. 500 g medium-grain couscous
  2. 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. 1 kg lamb, beef, or chicken (cut into pieces)
  4. 2 onions (chopped)
  5. 3 carrots
  6. 3 zucchini
  7. 2 turnips
  8. 2 tomatoes (peeled & grated)
  9. 1 can chickpeas (pre-cooked)
  10. 1 tsp ginger powder
  11. 1 tsp turmeric
  12. 1 tsp paprika
  13. Salt & pepper to taste
  14. Fresh coriander & parsley

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil and sauté onions until soft.
  2. Add the meat and brown it on all sides.
  3. Season with ginger, turmeric, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add grated tomatoes, chickpeas, and fresh herbs. Mix well.
  5. Pour enough water to cover the meat, bring to boil, then simmer for 45 minutes.
  6. Add carrots and turnips first, cook for 15 minutes.
  7. Add zucchini and continue cooking until vegetables are tender.
  8. Meanwhile, prepare the couscous:
    • Place couscous in a large bowl, sprinkle with water, and fluff with hands.
    • Steam the couscous in a couscoussier for 15–20 minutes.
    • Repeat the steaming process twice, moistening and fluffing each time.
  9. Arrange couscous on a large dish, make a well in the center, place meat and vegetables on top, and pour some broth.
  10. Serve hot with extra broth on the side.
